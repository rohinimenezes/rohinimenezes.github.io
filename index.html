My Food Adventures

A graduate students culinary adventures through the windy city

Hello! I'm Rohini and this page documents some of my favorite food adventures both in and out of the kitchen. I love to cook and always on the hunt for a new recipe to try at home. I also love trying out new restaurants in Chicago and hope this site can provide some inspiration for your next culinary adventure!

Recent Restaurant Reviews

Girl and the Goat

Great restaurant to celebrate a special occasion with a creative menu

Girl and the Goat is a Chicago staple and for good reason. This upscale, family style restaurant has an incredibly inventive menu which makes dining here more of an experience than a meal

Bandera

Delicious and comforting southwest cooking

Bandera is the perfect spot for dinner after a long day of shopping on Michigan Avenue. The cornbread skillet and ceviche are musts!

The Publican

A brunch spot that will never dissapoint!

Brunch and The Publican go hand in hand for most Chicagoans. This is the go-to spot for a satisfying and hearty brunch with a big group of people on a lazy Sunday.The place is packed on the weekends so make sure to get there early or prepare some fun games that you can play with your party while you wait for a table. It will be worth the wait!
